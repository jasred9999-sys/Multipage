Frequently Asked

How long does shipping take? +
Standard shipping takes 5–7 business days. Express (2–3 days) is available for ₹199. Same-day delivery is available in Hyderabad for ₹399.
What is your return policy? +
We offer free 30-day returns on all unused items in original packaging. Simply contact our support team and we'll arrange pickup from your door.
Are your products authentic? +
Every product is sourced directly from verified artisans and brands. We provide a certificate of authenticity with select items.
Do you ship internationally? +
Yes! We ship to 18+ countries. International shipping rates and timelines vary by destination and are calculated at checkout.
How do I track my order? +
Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking link via email and SMS. You can also track via your order confirmation number.
Do you offer wholesale or B2B? +
Yes, we have a dedicated B2B program for retailers and interior designers. Contact us at wholesale@luxestore.in for catalogue and pricing.
